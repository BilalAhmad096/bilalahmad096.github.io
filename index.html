About Me

I am joining the University of Bath for my PhD, building on my completed MPhil at Royal Holloway, University of London. My work focuses on Power Systems especially the integration of Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS), optimal operation, and Machine Learning enabled decision support for distribution networks.

Broadly, I’m interested in Power System Reliability, OPF/SCOPF, and Explainable AI for contingency screening and planning. This research aligns with the United Nations’ SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy and contributes to resilient, efficient, and low-carbon grids.
